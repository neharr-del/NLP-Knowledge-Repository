# Bag of Words

## Definition

Bag of Words converts text into numerical vectors based on word frequency.

---

## Example

Sentence

I love NLP

Vocabulary

I

Love

NLP

Machine

Vector

1 1 1 0

---

## Advantages

Simple

Fast

Easy implementation

---

## Disadvantages

Ignores word order

Cannot understand meaning

---

## Applications

Spam Detection

Text Classification

Sentiment Analysis
